About Hunter x Hunter

Hunter x Hunter is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. It follows the journey of Gon Freecs, a young boy who sets out to become a Hunter, a licensed professional who specializes in various dangerous and challenging tasks.

Main Characters

Gon Freecs

The protagonist of the story, a young boy with immense potential and a strong sense of justice.

Killua Zoldyck

Gon's best friend, a skilled assassin from a family of renowned assassins. He possesses incredible speed and agility.

Kurapika

A member of the Kurta Clan, a group that was massacred by the Phantom Troupe. He seeks revenge and possesses a powerful Nen ability called "Scarlet Eyes."

illumi Zoldyck

He is generally a stony, ruthless person with an extremely cold way of thinking, reflecting only his own interests. Still, he holds a twisted and overprotective form of love for his younger brother, Killua.

Leorio Paladiknight

A medical student who joins the Hunter Exam to become a doctor and fulfill his dream of helping others.

Hisoka Morow

Hisoka speaks little of his past because he is uninterested in it. It is revealed, however, that Hisoka joined the Phantom Troupe under false pretenses at some point after the Kurta massacre, and about two years before the events in Yorknew City.
